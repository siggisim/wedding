Daylight
The daylight in Iceland is from mid-May to mid-August and the sun only sets for around 3 hours per day, and there is effectively light for the whole 24-hour period.
Iceland
Alcohol
Alcohol is expensive in Iceland due to taxes levied based on the alcohol volume in a drink.

We recommend buying alcohol at the Duty Free store in the airport to avoid overpaying.

Check out this alcohol allowance calculator to decide how much you should buy.
